Born July 21st in Milwaukee, WI to Victor Rose Sr and Trenee Wells. Avid videogame player from 3-18. I'm still impressed that I beat Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy's Kong Quest at about 4 years old...I don't even think I could do that today. Huge Nintendo fan for a long time. Playstation is a great system also. Sypro Ripto's Rage was the first game I beat 100%. Crash bandicoot 3 Warped grabbing platinum medals is a great memory...plus the pizza hut demo discs giving me access to Taito titles. Good times.

Attended about 10 schools, most noteworth of them being Hartford Elemantary, Glen Hills Middle School, Riverside High, and University of Wisconsin-Madison. I studied Computer Engineering at University of Wisconsin and now I am currently building a portfolio of software development material, beginning with this website. My goals are to make videogames, websites, and apps, and eventually make a platform for people to get paid for their creative ventures. Like an Uber for artists. Like soundcloud, and tumblr combined. I want to own my own videogame studio that houses the umbrella for an all-creative company that handles music, teaches music, sells music, and rents out instruments.
